Projects developed in the Unity game engine

 

Released Games

Planet

Planet is a game about commanding a spaceship and exploring and fighting in a handcrafted solar system.

The player can upgrade their ship, command a fleet, and take on pirates while exploring space.

Spiderfell

Created for the Roachware 2025 game jam, Spiderfell is a game about navigating over 15 levels as a spider trying to eat ants.

The player clicks to shoot webs with the ultimate goal of making it to the queen ant at the very end.

Copy Cats

Created for the GMTK 2025 game jam, the player has to complete puzzles to reach the goal of the level while working alongside past copies of themselves.

Every 60 seconds, or when forcefully rewound, a copy of the player is created and that copy follows exactly what the player did before the loop.

Prototypes

Roguelike Dungeon

The player would explore a procedurally generated dungeon while fighting against various kinds of monsters in 3 different areas.

The color scheme of the game was totally monochrome with all textures being either 8x8 or 16x16.

Spaceship Autobattler

From a selection of 7 different ships, the player could set up spaceships to fight in battle.

The use of asynchronous programming allowed for thousands of laser pellets to be rendered all at once.
